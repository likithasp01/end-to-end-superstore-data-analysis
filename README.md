# Superstore Sales Data Analysis

## Problem Statement

Retail businesses generate large amounts of sales data every day. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify profitable products, high-performing regions, customer purchasing patterns, and areas that require improvement.

This project aims to analyze the Superstore dataset to uncover valuable business insights that can help improve sales performance, profitability, and decision-making using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset Source:**

Kaggle - Sample Superstore Dataset

The dataset contains information about:

- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity
- Discounts
- Shipping
- Regions
- Categories
- Sub-categories

## Key Findings

Some important insights discovered during the analysis include:

- Certain product categories contribute significantly more to total sales than others.
- High sales do not always translate into high profit due to heavy discounts.
- Regional sales performance varies considerably across different locations.
- A small number of customers contribute a large share of total revenue.
- Some products generate consistent losses despite high sales volume.
- Seasonal trends influence monthly sales performance.
